Black Gold
Business Intelligence Sales Performance Analysis

Black Gold retail store wants to design an end-to-end business intelligence solution from scratch. The company wants to track KPI on sales, profit, and profit margin, compare regional performance, analyze product level trends and forecasts, and identify high-value clients. The company provided a folder of raw CSV files containing information about transactions, products, customers, and territories. The dataset was extracted, uploaded, and transformed using Power BI analytical tools. Dimension tables were developed and connected to sales facts tables in the model view. DAX was deployed for calculations and two separate visualizations were built to analyze the company sales performance and demography. KPIs of total sales, total profit, total orders, and profit margin coupled with bookmark buttons were built and added to the dashboard. The clustered bar charts were used to analyze sales by category, top 10 products, and sales by region, and the line chart visualized the sales trend. The company management gets valued insights from the end-to-end business intelligence solution developed.
